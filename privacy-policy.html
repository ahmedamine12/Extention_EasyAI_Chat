Privacy Policy

EasyAI Chat Extension

Last updated: July 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This extension is designed with privacy in mind - your API keys and conversations are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Storage

This extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • API Keys: Your OpenAI and/or Google Gemini API keys (encrypted and stored locally)
  • Chat History: Your conversation history with AI assistants
  • Settings: Your extension preferences and settings
  • Extension State: UI state, position, and display preferences

1.2 No Server Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to our servers. All data remains on your local device.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Local Functionality

The extension uses your stored information to:

  • Authenticate with AI providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini)
  • Display your chat history
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide a personalized user experience

2.2 AI Provider Communication

When you use the extension to chat with AI:

  • Your messages are sent directly to the AI provider you've configured (OpenAI or Google Gemini)
  • These communications are subject to the respective provider's privacy policy
  • We do not have access to or store these communications

3. Data Security

3.1 Local Storage Security

Your data is stored using Chrome's secure local storage APIs. API keys are encrypted before storage for additional security.

3.2 No Network Transmission

Except for communications with AI providers (which you explicitly initiate), the extension does not transmit any data over the network.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 AI Providers

This extension integrates with:

  • OpenAI: For GPT model access
  • Google Gemini: For Gemini model access

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear chat history at any time through the extension interface
  • Remove API keys from the settings
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data

5.2 Extension Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings and chat history locally
  • Active Tab: To access page content for quick actions
  • Scripting: To inject the chat interface into web pages
  • Context Menus: To provide right-click quick actions
  • Host Permissions: To communicate with AI provider APIs

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Clear it through the extension interface
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the policy in the extension and on our website.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or website.
